Fun

  1. Football games!

    2. The tradition and spirit surrounding Notre Dame football is known nationwide, but there's nothing quite like taking part of the fun in the student section. Between the songs, cheers, and chants, there's nothing better than standing next to your friends cheering for an Irish win!

  2. Playing on the Quads!

    3. Notre Dame is one of the most beautifully landscaped schools in the country, in my biased opinion. There are several large quads throughout the campus that are perfect for playing with a frisbee, throwing a ball around, bringing study-time outside, or just lounging around. As long as the weather participates, you can find tons of Notre Dame students outside enjoying the quads!

  3. Clubs!

    4. There are hundreds of clubs just waiting for students to sign up for. You can have your choice, whether it's Spanish or Sailing, Cupcakes or Catholic Traditions, there's a club for everyone.
